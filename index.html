Introduction

As part of the project for the Web Games Development 2 module at the University of the West of Scotland we were required to make an online multiplayer game of our choosing. This game would then be embedded in a multifunctional webpage, also of our making, designed and built to promote and host the game and present it to the users with a design document assisted by pictures and a video demonstration. The website for the game is made up of four different pages. The first page contains a brief introduction accompanied by a slideshow of screenshots from the game and a short video demonstration of the gameplay. The second page is where the game is hosted and can be played from. The third page is then the page that contains the design document for the game, going over its influences, design and implementation. Finally, on the fourth page, the team members are presented alongside links on how to contact them.
